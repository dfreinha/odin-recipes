Baked Mostaccioli!!

HTML Image


Ingredients

  • 32 ounce of mostaccioli pasta
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 pound of hot Italian sausage
  • 3 jars of your favorite pasta sauce
  • 1 lbs of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Directions


  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add mostaccioli to the water and stir occasionally. Cook for 10 minutes then drain
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 F and grease baking pan
  3. Heat a nonstick pan to medium heat and add in ground beef and Italian sausage add a 1/4 cup of water to help cooking. Cook and stir ground beef until browned. Drain grease and add pasta sause
  4. Warm and stir meat sauce mixture over low heat for 3 minutes
  5. Add 1/2 of meat sause to baking dish, then add 1/2 of pasta over the sause, add 1/2 of mozzarella and parmesan to baking dish
  6. Add the remaining pasta on top of the cheese, cover pasta with the remaining sause, add the remaining chease to top
  7. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes
