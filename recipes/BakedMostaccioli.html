Baked Mostaccioli

The great recipe for meaty and cheesy baked mostaccioli.

Ingredients

  • 2 jars of Pasta sause
  • 1 lbs Ground beef
  • 1 lbs Spicy Italian sausage
  • 1 lbs Mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup Cheddar cheese
  • 2 boxs Penne pasta
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic salt

Directions

  1. Brown the ground beef and italian sausage in a pan and adding garlic salt
  2. Once the meat has browned stir in your favorite pasta sause adding Italian seasoning
  3. Grease a pan and place a layer on pasta in the pan
  4. On top of the layer of pasta add the cheddar chesse, on top of the cheddar cheese add a layer of Mozzarella cheese
  5. Pour half the the meat sause over the cheese
  6. Add the remaining half of the pasta over the sause
  7. Add another layer of Mozzarella over the pasta
  8. On top of the Mozzarella add the rest of the meat sause
  9. Add the remaining Mozzarella on top of the meat sause
  10. Cook at 350 degrees for 50 minutes
