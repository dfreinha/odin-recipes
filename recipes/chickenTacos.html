Chicken Tacos

A recipe for Chicken tacos.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp. Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1" strips
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp. chili powder
  • 2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp. paprika
  • 1/4 tsp. cayenne
  • Sour cream
  • Sliced red onion
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded Monterey Jack
  • Diced avocados
  • Lime wedges

Directions

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper and add to a skillet. Cook for 6 minutes. Add spices and cook for another minute.
  2. Warm tortillas and top with a layer of cheese.
  3. Build tacos: Serve and enjoy!
